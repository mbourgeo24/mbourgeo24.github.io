Boston area born

My name is Michael and I live in the Bay Area. Originally I am from about 15 minutes north of Boston, MA. My interest in programming developed while I was in the United States Air Force working for a think-tank named Phoenix Spark. The first program that I wrote was to turn LED lights off and on using Python and a Raspberry PI. After that I became hooked!. My goal is to be able to learn enough to leverage technologies to create complex back-end applications. Some of my interests include investing and bodybuilding which I think is a good Catalyst for programming. In both you have to be incredibly patient and consistent to see any form of improvement. You'll see some of my favorite projects that I've worked on showcased here!
